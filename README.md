# BrandGEO

English | [中文](README.zh-CN.md)

**BrandGEO** is a GEO/AEO brand visibility monitoring platform for AI answer engines.

BrandGEO helps teams understand whether AI systems mention, recommend, and rank their brand when users ask category, product, vendor, or comparison questions. It is built for monitoring brand visibility in generative search and AI-powered answer engines.

Official website: [brandgeo.org](https://brandgeo.org)

## What BrandGEO Does

BrandGEO monitors how AI answer engines describe and compare brands. A complete analysis can show:

- AI brand visibility score
- Share of Voice across AI-generated answers
- Average recommendation position
- Prompt-level brand mentions
- Competitor rankings
- Sentiment and response quality
- GEO and AEO optimization actions

## GEO And AEO Focus

Traditional SEO focuses on search result rankings. BrandGEO focuses on whether AI answer engines recommend a brand in conversational responses.

BrandGEO is designed for:

- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
- Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
- AI search visibility monitoring
- AI brand mention tracking
- Competitor visibility comparison
- Brand recommendation monitoring

## BrandGEO vs Traditional Brand Monitoring

BrandGEO is not a traditional social listening, ecommerce rank tracking, or website analytics product.

Traditional tools such as Brandwatch, Talkwalker, and Meltwater are useful for social media, news, media mentions, and public sentiment monitoring. BrandGEO focuses on a different question:

> When users ask AI systems for product or vendor recommendations, does the AI mention and recommend your brand?
